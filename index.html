Our Mission

Events

Children's Day

Every year on 14 November
“The children of today will make the India of tomorrow. The way we bring them up will determine the future of the country”

With this vision, we organize a memorable Children's Day where the bustling involvement of students in the spontaneous fun-filled games sparks off the day followed by a scintillating Open Dance Competition and a Talent Show where young dancers steal a chance to mesmerize the audience with their dynamics as they go across the floor.

Events

Teacher's Day

Every year on 5 September
Celebrating the guiding lights who shape minds and inspire futures on Teacher's Day.

The teacher's day is a very special day for both students as well as teachers, to make this day memorable, we organize several competitions like drawing, dance, quiz and many more. Students participated enthusiastically, it was a sight to behold!

Sunday Activity

Science Fair

Every year in January
“Coming together is a beginning, staying together is progress, and working together is a success.”

To mark the importance of National Science day, the we conduct an eminent science modeling competition where our passionate talented buds display the models and projects made by them to the visitors. Our teachers help these young innovators in developing their ideas into great projects and give a push-up to their creative minds.

Foundation Day

Every year on 5 September
"Marking the roots of our journey, Foundation Day is a celebration of growth, resilience, and the shared spirit that shapes our collective identity."

SGM was conceptualised in the 2010s with its sole purpose being to provide quality and free education to all. Since then, it has been an incredible journey of remarkable progress as well as significant learnings.Thus, to cherish these memories and to recollect the aim of this mission, we celebrate Founder's Day on 5th September every year.

SGM Family

" Families are like branches on a tree. We grow in different directions yet our roots remain as one. "

SGM is a movement to help students of Gwalior to get quality education irrespective of their financial background. As a part of this initative we conduct classes everyday where teachers and managers play an incrediable role to make everyday a successful day.

It's the hard work, dedication, and creativity of our team which makes every event amazing. To increase the efficiency, all members have been assigned clear roles and responsibilities, based on their interest, expertise and strengths. Communication within the team is open, transparent, and respectful, enabling effective collaboration and idea-sharing. We embrace constructive feedback, learn from mistakes, and continuously improve our performance.

SGM family is mentored by the Director of our esteemed institute Prof. S.N. Singh, Faculty Coordinator, Prof. Joydip Dhar (DOSA ABV-IIITM), and many other faculities.
